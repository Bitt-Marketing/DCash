DCash Wallet

The DCash application allows consumers to send and receive funds, purchase goods and services at local merchants, pay bills, and conveniently manage digital currency from their mobile device.

Anyone, with a smartphone, can download and signup to use DCash.

  • No Fees

    There are no hidden charges or fees for using DCash.

  • Crediblity

    DCash is issued and guaranteed by the ECCB.

  • Faster transactions

    DCash transactions are concluded faster than with cash or cards.

  • Cross Border Transfers

    DCash allows you to safely and instantly transfer large sums of money between individuals or businesses, locally or within the Eastern Caribbean Currency Union (ECCU).

  • Safety

    DCash exists in your digital wallet, accessible via your smartphone. Using DCash is safer than carrying large sums of money. If you lose your smartphone, you do NOT lose your DCash.

  • Contactless

    DCash is contactless. There is no required touching of other individuals or foreign objects.
