Exchanging digital currency and cash

Consumers can convert their digital currency to cash or purchase digital currency with cash through participating merchants. The DCashMerchant application allows two types of exchange transactions:

  • Cash in Exchange - The consumer gives the merchant cash in exchange for an equal amount of digital currency.

  • Cash Out Exchange - The merchant gives the consumer cash in exchange for an equal amount of digital currency.

Exchange digital currency for cash

  1. Tap Exchange at the bottom of the screen.

  2. Tap Cash Out Exchange.

  3. Enter the amount of digital currency the consumer is exchanging for cash.

  4. Tap Confirm Amount.

  5. A QR code is generated. The consumer scans this using their DCash Wallet application.

  6. Tap Done.

Exchange cash for digital currency

  1. Tap Exchange at the bottom of the screen.

  2. Tap Cash In Exchange.

  3. Enter the amount of cash you are receiving from the consumer.

  4. Tap Continue.

  5. If the consumer has generated a QR code on their device, you can simply scan the code with your mobile device.

  6. On the next screen, tap Continue.

  7. On the Complete Exchange screen, make sure that the recipient and amount of the cash you are receiving is correct and add an optional note.

  8. Slide to Send.
